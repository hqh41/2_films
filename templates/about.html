Game instructions


Hello, and welcome to « Who’s the actor ?!»

Do you want to test your knowledge about movies and actors ? You found the right game ! This game is really easy to play : You have to guess the actor in common for the two films that are displayed to you. Very simple, right? To play, after choosing your level of difficulty between easy, normal and hard, the game will start. Each game contains 10 questions, with every correct answer you will gain points according to the rules below : To each question, you have 3 ways of answering : 50:50, square, or shotgun. If you select 50:50, you will have 2 propositions, and if your answer is correct you win 1 point. If you select square, you will have 4 propositions, and if your answer is correct you win 3 points. If you select shotgun, you will have to write the actor's name, and if your answer is correct you win 5 points. At the game's end, you'll have the total of all your points, and if your score is high enough you will see your name in the top10. Think you can be the best? Game on!
